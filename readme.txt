Split Email Providers is a WooCommerce addon that automates email sending to suppliers for every order, simplifying communication.

== Description ==

🌟 **Split Email Providers** is an innovative WooCommerce addon that optimizes order tracking for store managers dealing with multiple suppliers. This addon sends automated emails for each order to the corresponding supplier. No need to go through a marketplace, making the process simpler, faster, and more efficient. Perfect for businesses aiming to maintain a direct relationship with their suppliers while using WooCommerce.

Key Features:
* 📬 **Automated email sending to suppliers**: For every order, emails are sent to the relevant suppliers with product details.  
* 🔄 **Seamless integration**: Works directly with WooCommerce without requiring complex configuration.  

== Installation ==

1. **Download the addon** from the WordPress repository or directly from our website.  
2. **Go to your WordPress dashboard**, click on "Plugins > Add New" and upload the file.  
3. **Activate the addon**, assign your products to suppliers, and start sending automated emails to the relevant suppliers.  

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= 🛠️ Is this addon exclusively for WooCommerce? =  
Yes, Split Email Providers is specifically designed for WooCommerce stores.  

= 📝 Can the content of the emails sent to suppliers be customized? =  
No, the addon does not currently include options to customize the email message or details.
